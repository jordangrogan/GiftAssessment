Spiritual Gifts & Personality Assessment

We believe God has made you for a purpose! Finding and fulfilling your God-given purpose is one of the greatest discoveries you will make in life. This online SHAPE process is a tool to help you discern your unique SHAPE and empower you to connect in a meaningful place of service. The Bible tells us, “we are God’s handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do.”

SHAPE stands for Spiritual Gifts, Heart, Abilities, Personality, and Experience. Although there are many paths to discovering your best place of service, the online SHAPE assessment is one way to learn more about the role you can play in God’s grand story!

Take some time to reflect and answer the following questions. If you don't have an answer right away that's fine, just write "Not Sure". You'll have an opportunity to discuss this further with a coach later! Please allow at least 45 minutes to take the full assessment. We are aware that it can seem pretty lengthy, but each portion of the assessment is vital to helping us help you evaluate your “SHAPE,” and then to help you find a place of ministry.

We hope you will find this survey helpful. Please be assured that we keep all information confidential and will not violate your privacy in any way. This assessment is based on Saddleback Church’s S.H.A.P.E. Assessment.
